## User Stories/Journey

* Sign Up - for new users
* Log in - for existing users
* For new users - after registraion: Create profile first before viewing your profile.
* After creating your profile, you can now join a neighbhourhood, if your neighbourhood doesn't exist, you can create one.
* After joining a neighbourhood, you can view, and add posts, and businusses, in your neighbourhood; you can also see the available government departments.
* You can click on the cover photo of either a business or a department for a modal with details
* You can also comment on other users' posts
* Enjoy The App.
